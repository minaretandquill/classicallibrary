## Quick Upload Guide

1. **Create folder** in Plesk: `httpdocs/book_covers/`
2. **Upload** `pendingfrontcover.jpg` (required for all pending books)
3. **Upload** individual book covers with exact filenames above
4. **Refresh** website - images appear automatically!

**No rebuild needed** when adding images - just upload correctly named files.
